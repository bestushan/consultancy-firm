About Me

Ushantha Attygalle

I am a seasoned consultant and trainer specializing in leadership development, organizational culture transformation, and team performance enhancement.

With a rich background that includes working with diverse organizations across multiple countries, I bring a global perspective to local challenges. My approach combines evidence-based methodologies with practical, real-world applications.

I hold multiple certifications in leadership development and organizational psychology, and I'm committed to helping organizations unlock their full potential through their people.
